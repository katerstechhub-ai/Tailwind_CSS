TWITTER

Known for real-time news and public discourse, it is a micro-blogging service where users share short updates, polls, and threads. It is characterized by brevity and is often used as a "second screen" during live events

INSTAGRAM

A visually-driven platform centered on high-quality storytelling through images and videos. Key features include Reels for short-form entertainment, Stories for temporary updates, and a curated grid for a cohesive brand aesthetic.

YOUTUBE

Owned by Google, it is the world's second-largest search engine and the primary destination for long-form video content. It serves as a tool for learning, education, and entertainment, allowing creators to build dedicated subscriber bases.

LINKEDIN

The premier site for professional networking and career development. It is used for job searching, sharing industry insights, and building business credibility through professional conversations rather than casual social interaction
